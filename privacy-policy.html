✅ This app does not collect any personal data

This Privacy Policy describes how LockApp ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information. LockApp is a PIN lock screen application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or registration is required
  • No personal data is collected

  • No camera or microphone access
  • No contacts or files are accessed

2. PIN Storage

The PIN used to unlock the app is stored only on your device using your browser's local storage. It is never sent to any server or third party.

3. Internet Access

The app uses internet access solely to load web fonts (Google Fonts) for display purposes. No personal data is transmitted.

4. Third-Party Services

LockApp uses Google Fonts to display text. Google Fonts may collect limited technical data (such as IP address) per their own privacy policy. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for Google's data practices.

5. Children's Privacy

LockApp does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no content inappropriate for any age.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.
